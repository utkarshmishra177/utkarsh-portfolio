About

Utkarsh Mishra

I am a journalist and writer based in Ranchi, Jharkhand. My reporting covers labour rights and the informal economy, climate and environmental policy, land and Adivasi rights, and constitutional and institutional governance — with sustained attention to how structural policy failures land hardest on people at the margins.

Before writing independently, I worked at Aajeevika Bureau on migrant labour rights — an experience that continues to shape how I approach questions of informality, welfare, and working-class life in India. My work combines field-level sourcing with verified government, judicial, and research data, and is published across progressive and mainstream outlets simultaneously.
