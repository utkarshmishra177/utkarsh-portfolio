About

Utkarsh
Mishra

Published in
Countercurrents Feminism in India Down to Earth The India Forum Medium Chintan / India Foundation Zee News

I am a journalist and writer based in Ranchi, Jharkhand. My reporting covers land rights, labour, Adivasi and tribal communities, urban housing, climate justice, and international law — with sustained attention to how structural policy failures land hardest on people at the margins.

My work has appeared in Countercurrents, Feminism in India, Down to Earth, The India Forum, Chintan (India Foundation), and Zee News, among others. I report across progressive and mainstream publications simultaneously, treating journalism as both analysis and advocacy.

Before writing independently, I worked at Aajeevika Bureau on migrant labour rights — an experience that continues to shape how I approach questions of informality, welfare, and working-class life in India.
